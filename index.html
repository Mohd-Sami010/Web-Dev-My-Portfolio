In this game, you have a M4 assualt rifle and an Antidote gun containing 10 antidote shots. You task is to treat the Zombies (by shooting antidote towards them) lurking around in city, once you shoot the zombie with antidote gun, it will stop moving for some seconds and converts to a fit person, then escort them to camp and giving them ommands like Follow me, Stop and Go inside the camp. After they enter the camp and the evacuation truck, it will come and evacuate the people inside the camp you get money from it that you can use order more antidote, rifle bullets and medkit.
